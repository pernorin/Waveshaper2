Vi är medlem av aktade "Europa Cinemas

Europa cinemas founded in 1992 with funding from the MEDIA Programme (Creative Europe) and from the Centre national du cinéma et de l'image animée (CNC), Europa Cinemas is the first film theatre network focusing on European films. It now has 1,232 cinemas (3,123 screens) in 751 cities and 43 countries.
